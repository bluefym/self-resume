The Photographer

Hello! I'm Prem. I take photos of random stuff in my free time, mostly of sunsets, cars and other eye catching subjects. Photography is my way of expressing how I see the world around me. I can spend hours waiting for the perfect light, the perfect angle, the perfect moment to click that one shot that tells a story. Whether its a quick snapshot with a high shutter speed or a long exposure capturing the dance of city lights, I strive to create images that evoke emotion and spark imagination.

I usually shoot with my Canon EOS 5D Mark III paired with a 18-55mm kit. Nothing too special, but it's the perfect companion to capture photons. I also shoot on my phone- an iQOO Neo 7 sometimes. When I'm not behind the camera, I enjoy exploring new places, meeting fellow photography enthusiasts, and sharing my work with the world. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or just chat about all things photography.
